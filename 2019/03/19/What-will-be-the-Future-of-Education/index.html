What will be the Future of Education?

Posted on 2019-03-19   |   In Technology   |  

As a Master student, I have been educated for over 20 years. In these 20 years, the way of education has changed a lot. However, in the next 20 years, the changes in the education industry will be even more significant. If you are interested in what will the future of education be, please follow me and we will first focus on the converging technologies that have great impacts on education, then talk about the role of the teacher in the future and finally what education will be in 10 to 20 years?

Converging Technologies

Just like other industries, technology is a crucial factor in the change in the education industry. Among the bunch of techniques, I chose Internet Technologies, Virtual Reality (VR)/Augmented Reality (AR) and Artificial Intelligence (AI) to be the most influential ones.

Internet Technologies

Maybe many people ignore the impact of the Internet on education because we are used to living in an environment that the Internet is everywhere. However, I remembered that about 15 years ago, in my elementary school, the classroom is not equipped with electronic devices at all. It was unimaginable to teach students abroad by a real-time video sharing course. This is the power of the Internet. Online education, especially massive open online course (MOOC) has liberated education from the limit of the region. The students from all over the world can learn the same course of justice at Harvard on edX. In the future, the Internet will still be the central supporting technology for modern education.

Virtual Reality/Augmented Reality

VR and AR come from the domain of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI). VR gives us an immersive user experience, and AR mixes the computer-generated perceptual information with the real-world environment. They both change the way we interact with the environment and particularly, how we see things. Traditionally, we learn knowledge from words, pictures, and videos. However, using VR/AR, we can learn the structure of the human body by just watching a “real” model of the human body, and we can “go” to the tropical rainforest in the VR headset in the geology class. Nowadays, VR/AR technologies are becoming more mature, and they will bring students a more enjoyable and experience-based learning environment.

Artificial Intelligence

AI is the most powerful technology in the past five years, and education can be changed totally by it. First, AI can automate many basic activities in education, like grading. This can be super helpful because these activities usually cost teachers a lot of time. What’s more, educational software can be adapted to student needs. AI can learn how much you have understood one topic and teach you again in the points you might have some doubts. This is impossible for teachers to do this because one teacher usually faces tens of students. Last but not least, AI can take over the role of teaching in most of the cases and it is more stable than human teachers because AI will never forget something or influenced by bad mood. It could be foreseen that AI will replace a big portion of teachers’ work now.

Will Teachers Disappear?

So with these modern technologies, will the role of teacher disappear? My answer is no. Technical speaking, the role of the teacher does not require to be human beings. We can call AI teachers our teachers, too. So even if we do not have human teachers at all in the future, we cannot say that teachers are disappeared. I think robots or AI will replace a lot of teaching work in elementary school and middle school in the future. However, the work of professors in universities is not easy to be replaced because their work is to expand the boundaries of human knowledge. So technologies will free teachers’ hands on the repetitive work and let them focus more on creative work. Also, we need to think about what is the critical value that we can not learn from AI.

The Future of Education

Internet technologies will continuously bring convenient into education, connecting people all over the world. AR and VR will let us learn by experience and make the education process more interesting. AI will replace a lot of teachers’ work and provide us personalized education service. These technologies will not replace teachers or school, but they will let us think about what is the most important and not replaceable in education. The way of learning will change, but the nature that we get to know the world around us by continuous learning will not change.

Thank you for your reading and see you next time.
